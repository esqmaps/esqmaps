How it works

From lost to licensed

A free consultation and a few targeted sessions is usually all it takes.

01

Diagnose

We work through real MBE questions and MEE essays together in a live 1:1 session. Emma identifies the exact reasoning errors you are making, not just the wrong answers, and builds a picture of where your thinking breaks down.

02

Drill and pressure-test

Every session after the diagnosis includes a 15-minute timed essay window, written under real exam conditions. That window is complimentary. Then you spend 30 minutes in a paid debrief with Emma: breaking down what held, what collapsed, and exactly why. It is the closest thing to the real exam before the real exam.

03

Pass

Every subject gets mapped to how you think. You walk into exam day with a system built for your brain — not memorized rules that dissolve under pressure. Every session is 1:1, every strategy is yours.
